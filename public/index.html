Welcome to Emotion Tinder!

Our application offers you an innovative dating experience built on state-of-the-art Eyetracking Technology.

Your journey begins

With the initial stage where a selection of dating profile pictures is presented to you. With each round, we display a set of nine images.

View and swipe

Your task is simply to view these pictures. Utilizing our Eyetracking Technology, we track the images that capture your attention the most.

Swipe left or right

In the second stage, we showcase the most intriguing pictures again. Here, it's up to you to swipe left if you like a profile or swipe right if it doesn’t match your preferences.

Calibration is crucial

To ensure the effectiveness of the Eyetracking, we kindly ask you to sit in a well-lit room and position your face within the designated rectangle on your camera.

Fine-tuning for accuracy

We also request you to perform the calibration initially to fine-tune the Eyetracking Technology for optimal performance.
